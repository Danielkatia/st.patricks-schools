About Us

We are a private school located in Mbiuni,Mwala sub-county,Machakos county

Our school is a catholic based and therefore christian doctrines are upheld highly so as to ensure quality in terms of service to God is realised we know that if we honour Him we are guaranteed of success. We emphasise on discipline as the main aspect of all our disciplines of the school as we know that it is the only thing that is applicable in all areas of life. We also help the learners on how to become independen in making decisions in their daily life.
